Use Case Name: Analyzing Review Data for Target words and Phrases

Scenario: A publishing company realized the impact that reviews have on book sales. Locating review data and prepping it for analysis is their initial task.
The follow-on task is for analysis of the review data for isolation of repetitive words and phrases.

Context: Isolate the most frequent words and sentences customers use in 5 star ratings of books.

Scope: Given the preprocessed 5 star review data analyze and isolate the most used words and phrases.

Success Condition:
Generate reports based on the most used words and phrases in the Amazon book review data.
