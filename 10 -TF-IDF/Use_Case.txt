Use Case Name: Analyze the weight and relevance of terms across a number of files

Scenario: A political campaign wanted to analyze the speeches of an opponent to isolate keywords based on frequency and weight across multiple files.

Context: By isolating keywords for frequency and weight in any given collection of speeches it provides one with a wider picture of allies or opponents for consideration in future debates or advertising.

Scope: Given a collection of speeches isolate keywords that the politician uses most and by weight.


************IMPORTANT*********
This is an example case, For extreme clarity I would like to restate THIS IS AN EXAMPLE CASE using test speeches from President Obama.
The collection of speeches used in this exercise were gathered from (https://www.americanrhetoric.com/)

Success Condition:
Generate reports based on the term frequency across multiple documents.
