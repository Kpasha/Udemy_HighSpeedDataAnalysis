Use Case Name: Extract Text from Images

Scenario: Social Media analyst wants to be able to extract as much text as possible from images appearing in their posts and feed.

Context: In an increased effort to protect the brand, they would like to be more diligent in the reporting and removal of any offensive memes or images.

Scope: Given a variety of image types, text needs to be extracted for further analysis.

Success Condition:
Create files with the text extracted for images provided for later analysis.

Extra: Generate a report containing image name and extracted text to share with team members.
