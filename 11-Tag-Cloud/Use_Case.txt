Use Case Name: Generate a word cloud from book reviews

Scenario: A publishing company realized the impact that reviews have on book sales. Locating review data and prepping it for analysis is their initial task.
The follow-on task is for analysis of the review data for isolation of repetitive words and phrases. The publisher wanted a visual quick ref of prominent words from the 5 star review text.

Context: Isolate the most used words and sentences customers use in 5 star ratings of books. Display them prominently where size is determined by frequency of occurrence.

Scope: Given the preprocessed 5 star review data analyze and isolate the most used words and phrases.


Success Condition:
Generate a image file with a word cloud based on the most used words in the Amazon book review data.
